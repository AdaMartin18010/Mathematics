## 概念定义 | Concept Definition

### 中断机制 | Interruption Mechanism

- 中文定义：中断机制是指在知识库构建过程中，为了保持内容质量和系统性，在特定节点暂停当前任务，记录进度状态，以便后续恢复和继续推进的系统化方法。
- English Definition: An interruption mechanism is a systematic approach in knowledge base construction where current tasks are paused at specific nodes to maintain content quality and systematicity, recording progress status for subsequent resumption and continuation.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO 9001:2015 (Quality management systems)
  - IEEE 830-1998 (Software requirements specifications)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Knowledge Management
  - Wikipedia: Interruption management
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 中断机制虽然保证了质量，但可能影响工作的连续性
  - 需要在效率和质量之间找到平衡

### 持续执行 | Continuous Execution

- 中文定义：持续执行是指在知识库构建过程中，通过系统化的方法和工具，确保内容构建的连续性和完整性，支持长期、可持续的知识积累和演化。
- English Definition: Continuous execution refers to the systematic methods and tools in knowledge base construction that ensure the continuity and completeness of content building, supporting long-term, sustainable knowledge accumulation and evolution.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO 56000:2020 (Innovation management)
  - IEEE 12207:2017 (Software lifecycle processes)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Continuous Learning
  - Wikipedia: Continuous execution
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 持续执行虽然保证了连续性，但可能缺乏反思和优化
  - 需要在稳定性和创新性之间找到平衡

### 知识图谱 | Knowledge Graph

- 中文定义：知识图谱是一种以图结构组织和表达知识的技术，通过节点和边表示实体及其关系，支持推理和智能应用，是现代知识管理的重要工具。
- English Definition: A knowledge graph is a technology for organizing and representing knowledge in a graph structure, using nodes and edges to represent entities and their relationships, supporting reasoning and intelligent applications, an important tool for modern knowledge management.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Knowledge Graphs
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Knowledge Representation
  - Wikipedia: Knowledge graph
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 知识图谱虽然提供了结构化表示，但可能简化了知识的复杂性
  - 知识表示的形式化与灵活性之间存在张力

### 递归扩展 | Recursive Expansion

- 中文定义：递归扩展是指在知识库构建中，通过自相似的结构和方法，不断深化和细化知识内容，实现知识的层次化和系统化发展。
- English Definition: Recursive expansion refers to the deepening and refinement of knowledge content through self-similar structures and methods in knowledge base construction, achieving hierarchical and systematic development of knowledge.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Recursion
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Recursive Functions
  - Wikipedia: Recursion
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 递归扩展虽然提供了系统性，但可能陷入无限细化的困境
  - 需要在深度和广度之间找到平衡

**可计算性框架：**
```lean
-- 图灵机的基本结构
inductive TuringMachine : Type
| mk : (states : List State) → 
      (alphabet : List Symbol) → 
      (transition : State → Symbol → State × Symbol × Direction) →
      (initial : State) → 
      (accept : List State) → 
      TuringMachine

-- 可计算性定义
def computable : (string → string) → Prop
| f => exists (tm : TuringMachine),
      forall (input : string), 
        tm_computes tm input (f input)
```
