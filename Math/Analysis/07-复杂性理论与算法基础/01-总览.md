## 概念定义 | Concept Definition

### 复杂性理论与算法基础 | Complexity Theory & Algorithmic Foundations

- 中文定义：复杂性理论与算法基础是研究计算问题内在难度、资源消耗和高效求解方法的理论体系。复杂性理论关注问题的计算难度边界，算法基础关注高效求解的方法论，两者共同构成了现代计算机科学和人工智能的理论基础。
- English Definition: Complexity theory and algorithmic foundations are theoretical frameworks that study the intrinsic difficulty, resource consumption, and efficient solution methods of computational problems. Complexity theory focuses on the computational difficulty boundaries of problems, while algorithmic foundations focus on methodologies for efficient solutions, together forming the theoretical foundation of modern computer science and artificial intelligence.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Computational Complexity Theory, Algorithmic Foundations
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics (Springer): Complexity theory, Algorithmic foundations
  - Wikipedia: Computational complexity theory, Algorithmic foundations
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 复杂性理论与算法基础的发展经历了从图灵机到现代复杂性理论的漫长历程，P=NP问题揭示了计算的本质限制。
  - 不同复杂性类（P、NP、PSPACE、EXPTIME等）反映了不同的计算能力和哲学立场。
  - 现代AI与量子计算将复杂性理论与算法基础推向新的前沿，但"计算的本质""人机协作"等仍是哲学挑战。

### 复杂性理论 | Complexity Theory

- 中文定义：复杂性理论研究计算问题的内在难度及其资源消耗（如时间、空间），是理论计算机科学的核心分支，揭示了算法的效率边界。
- English Definition: Complexity theory studies the intrinsic difficulty of computational problems and their resource consumption (such as time and space), a core branch of theoretical computer science that reveals the efficiency boundaries of algorithms.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Computational Complexity Theory
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Complexity theory
  - Wikipedia: Computational complexity theory
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 复杂性理论虽然深刻，但可能面临P=NP问题等根本挑战
  - 理论复杂性与实际效率之间存在张力

### 算法基础 | Algorithmic Foundations

- 中文定义：算法基础关注高效求解问题的方法与理论，包括算法设计、分析、优化等核心内容，为计算机科学提供理论基础。
- English Definition: Algorithmic foundations focus on methods and theories for efficiently solving problems, including algorithm design, analysis, optimization, and other core content, providing theoretical foundations for computer science.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Algorithmic Foundations
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Algorithmic foundations
  - Wikipedia: Algorithmic foundations
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 算法基础虽然实用，但可能面临设计权衡问题
  - 理论最优与实际可行之间存在张力

### P类问题 | P Class Problems

- 中文定义：P类问题是指可以在多项式时间内被确定性图灵机解决的问题，被认为是"高效可解"的问题类。
- English Definition: P class problems are problems that can be solved by deterministic Turing machines in polynomial time, considered the class of "efficiently solvable" problems.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: P Class Problems
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: P class problems
  - Wikipedia: P (complexity)
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - P类问题虽然高效，但可能面临实际效率问题
  - 理论效率与实际效率之间存在张力

### NP类问题 | NP Class Problems

- 中文定义：NP类问题是指可以在多项式时间内被非确定性图灵机验证的问题，是复杂性理论中的核心概念。
- English Definition: NP class problems are problems that can be verified by non-deterministic Turing machines in polynomial time, a core concept in complexity theory.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: NP Class Problems
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: NP class problems
  - Wikipedia: NP (complexity)
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - NP类问题虽然广泛，但可能面临P=NP问题等根本挑战
  - 验证效率与求解效率之间存在张力

### NP完全问题 | NP-Complete Problems

- 中文定义：NP完全问题是NP类中最困难的问题，如果任何一个NP完全问题可以在多项式时间内解决，则所有NP问题都可以在多项式时间内解决。
- English Definition: NP-complete problems are the most difficult problems in the NP class. If any NP-complete problem can be solved in polynomial time, then all NP problems can be solved in polynomial time.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: NP-Complete Problems
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: NP-complete problems
  - Wikipedia: NP-completeness
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - NP完全问题虽然困难，但可能面临量子计算等新技术的挑战
  - 理论困难与实际求解之间存在张力
