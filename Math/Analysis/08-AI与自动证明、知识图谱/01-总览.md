## 概念定义 | Concept Definition

### AI与自动证明、知识图谱 | AI & Automated Theorem Proving, Knowledge Graphs

- 中文定义：AI与自动证明、知识图谱是研究人工智能技术在数学证明、知识表示和推理中应用的理论体系。自动定理证明关注形式化证明的自动化，知识图谱关注结构化知识的表示和推理，两者共同构成了现代数学和人工智能的重要前沿。
- English Definition: AI and automated theorem proving, knowledge graphs are theoretical frameworks that study the application of artificial intelligence technology in mathematical proof, knowledge representation, and reasoning. Automated theorem proving focuses on the automation of formal proofs, while knowledge graphs focus on the representation and reasoning of structured knowledge, together forming important frontiers of modern mathematics and artificial intelligence.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Automated Theorem Proving, Knowledge Graphs, Artificial Intelligence
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics (Springer): Automated theorem proving, Knowledge graphs, Artificial intelligence
  - Wikipedia: Automated theorem proving, Knowledge graph, Artificial intelligence
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - AI与自动证明、知识图谱的发展经历了从符号推理到神经符号集成的漫长历程，大语言模型为数学证明提供了新的可能性。
  - 不同证明方法（形式化证明、交互式证明、神经符号推理等）反映了不同的数学哲学和计算范式。
  - 现代AI与量子计算将自动证明和知识图谱推向新的前沿，但"证明的可解释性""人机协作"等仍是哲学挑战。

### 人工智能 | Artificial Intelligence

- 中文定义：人工智能是研究使计算机系统能够执行通常需要人类智能的任务的科学，包括机器学习、自然语言处理、计算机视觉等分支。
- English Definition: Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the science of enabling computer systems to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, including machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and other branches.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Artificial Intelligence
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Artificial intelligence
  - Wikipedia: Artificial intelligence

  - 机器智能与人类智能之间存在张力

### 自动定理证明 | Automated Theorem Proving

- 中文定义：自动定理证明是指使用计算机程序自动生成数学定理证明的过程，是形式化数学和计算机科学的重要分支。
- English Definition: Automated theorem proving is the process of using computer programs to automatically generate mathematical theorem proofs, an important branch of formal mathematics and computer science.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Automated Theorem Proving
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Automated theorem proving
  - Wikipedia: Automated theorem proving
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 自动定理证明虽然高效，但可能面临可解释性问题
  - 自动化证明与人类理解之间存在张力

### 知识图谱 | Knowledge Graph

- 中文定义：知识图谱是一种结构化的知识表示方法，通过图结构表示实体、概念及其关系，支持语义搜索和智能推理。
- English Definition: A knowledge graph is a structured knowledge representation method that represents entities, concepts, and their relationships through graph structures, supporting semantic search and intelligent reasoning.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Knowledge Graphs
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Knowledge graphs
  - Wikipedia: Knowledge graph
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 知识图谱虽然结构化，但可能面临知识完整性问题
  - 结构化表示与知识复杂性之间存在张力

### 神经符号推理 | Neuro-symbolic Reasoning

- 中文定义：神经符号推理是结合神经网络和符号推理的混合方法，旨在实现可解释的智能推理。
- English Definition: Neuro-symbolic reasoning is a hybrid method that combines neural networks and symbolic reasoning, aiming to achieve interpretable intelligent reasoning.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)

- 中文定义：大语言模型是基于深度学习的自然语言处理模型，能够理解和生成人类语言，在数学推理中发挥重要作用。
- English Definition: Large language models are deep learning-based natural language processing models that can understand and generate human language, playing an important role in mathematical reasoning.

  - 生成能力与准确性之间存在张力

### 归结原理 | Resolution Principle

- 中文定义：归结原理是自动定理证明中的核心推理规则，通过消解互补文字来证明逻辑公式的有效性。
- English Definition: The resolution principle is a core inference rule in automated theorem proving that proves the validity of logical formulas by resolving complementary literals.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Resolution Principle
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Resolution principle
  - Wikipedia: Resolution (logic)
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 归结原理虽然有效，但可能面临搜索空间爆炸问题
  - 推理效率与完备性之间存在张力

### SMT求解 | SMT Solving

- 中文定义：SMT（可满足性模理论）求解是结合布尔可满足性和理论推理的自动推理方法，广泛应用于形式化验证。
- English Definition: SMT (Satisfiability Modulo Theories) solving is an automated reasoning method that combines Boolean satisfiability and theoretical reasoning, widely used in formal verification.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: SMT Solving
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: SMT solving
  - Wikipedia: Satisfiability modulo theories
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - SMT求解虽然强大，但可能面临理论组合问题
  - 求解能力与理论复杂性之间存在张力
