## 概念定义 | Concept Definition

### 人工智能 | Artificial Intelligence

- 中文定义：人工智能是研究使计算机系统能够执行通常需要人类智能的任务的科学，包括机器学习、自然语言处理、计算机视觉等分支。
- English Definition: Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the science of enabling computer systems to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, including machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and other branches.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Artificial Intelligence
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Artificial Intelligence
  - Wikipedia: Artificial intelligence
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - AI推动了数学、科学与工程的自动化，但也带来可解释性、可靠性等挑战
  - 强AI与弱AI的哲学争议仍在持续

### 自动定理证明 | Automated Theorem Proving

- 中文定义：自动定理证明是利用计算机自动验证或发现数学证明的方法，是形式化验证和数学基础研究的重要工具。
- English Definition: Automated theorem proving refers to the use of computers to automatically verify or discover mathematical proofs, an important tool for formal verification and mathematical foundations research.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Automated Theorem Proving
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Automated theorem proving
  - Wikipedia: Automated theorem proving
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 自动证明虽然提高了效率，但可能缺乏数学直觉和创造性
  - 证明的可读性和可理解性是重要挑战

### 知识图谱 | Knowledge Graph

- 中文定义：知识图谱是一种以图结构组织和表达知识的技术，支持推理与智能应用，是现代AI系统的重要组成部分。
- English Definition: A knowledge graph is a technology for organizing and representing knowledge in a graph structure, supporting reasoning and intelligent applications, an important component of modern AI systems.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Knowledge Graphs
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Knowledge Graph
  - Wikipedia: Knowledge graph
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 知识图谱的本体论、语义一致性与动态演化是持续研究的难题
  - 知识表示的形式化与灵活性之间存在张力

### 神经符号推理 | Neuro-Symbolic Reasoning

- 中文定义：神经符号推理是结合神经网络和符号推理的AI方法，试图融合神经网络的表示学习能力和符号系统的逻辑推理能力。
- English Definition: Neuro-symbolic reasoning is an AI approach that combines neural networks and symbolic reasoning, attempting to integrate the representation learning capabilities of neural networks with the logical reasoning capabilities of symbolic systems.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Neural-Symbolic Integration
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Neural-Symbolic Reasoning
  - Wikipedia: Neural-symbolic AI
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 神经符号方法试图解决AI的可解释性问题
  - 符号与神经网络的融合仍面临理论和实践挑战

### 3.1 罗宾逊与归结原理 | Robinson & Resolution

**代表人物与贡献：**

- 约翰·艾伦·罗宾逊（John Alan Robinson, 1930-2016）
- 发展了归结原理
- 为自动定理证明奠定基础

**原话引用：**
> "The resolution principle is a complete inference rule for first-order logic."
> "归结原理是一阶逻辑的完备推理规则。" — 罗宾逊

**归结原理形式化：**

```lean
-- 归结原理的形式化定义
def resolution : Clause → Clause → Clause → Prop
| C1, C2, C3 => 
  exists (L1 ∈ C1) (L2 ∈ C2),
  L1 = not L2 ∧ C3 = (C1 - {L1}) ∪ (C2 - {L2})
```
