This topic focuses on AI-assisted proofs, automated theorem proving, neuro-symbolic reasoning, and knowledge graphs, emphasizing AI applications in mathematical reasoning, automation of formal proofs, integration of neural networks and symbolic systems, and structured methods for knowledge representation and reasoning.

## 概念定义 | Concept Definition

### AI与自动证明、知识图谱 | AI, Automated Proofs & Knowledge Graphs

- 中文定义：AI与自动证明、知识图谱是研究人工智能技术在数学证明自动化、知识表示与推理中的应用的交叉领域，探索如何利用计算机系统自动或辅助生成数学证明、表示结构化知识并进行智能推理。AI辅助证明研究如何利用人工智能技术辅助或自动化数学证明过程，知识图谱则提供了表示和组织知识的结构化框架，两者结合为数学推理的自动化与智能化提供了新途径。
- English Definition: AI, automated proofs, and knowledge graphs constitute an interdisciplinary field studying the application of artificial intelligence technologies in automating mathematical proofs, knowledge representation, and reasoning, exploring how computer systems can automatically or assistively generate mathematical proofs, represent structured knowledge, and perform intelligent reasoning. AI-assisted proofs research how to leverage artificial intelligence technologies to assist or automate the mathematical proof process, while knowledge graphs provide a structured framework for representing and organizing knowledge, their combination offering new approaches for the automation and intelligence of mathematical reasoning.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ACM Computing Classification System: Artificial intelligence > Knowledge representation and reasoning
  - IEEE Standard Glossary of Software Engineering Terminology
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Automated Reasoning, Knowledge Representation
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics (Springer): Automated theorem proving, Knowledge representation
  - Wikipedia: Automated theorem proving, Knowledge graph
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - AI与自动证明、知识图谱的发展挑战了传统数学证明的本质，引发了关于数学创造性、理解与机械化证明的深刻哲学讨论。
  - 不同自动证明方法（如基于模型检验、基于演绎的方法）反映了不同的推理范式和数学哲学观点。
  - 现代大型语言模型与神经符号系统正在改变我们对数学推理能力的认识，但"理解与符号操作的区别""数学直觉的本质"等仍是核心哲学问题。

### 自动定理证明 | Automated Theorem Proving

- 中文定义：自动定理证明是利用计算机程序自动生成或验证数学命题证明的领域，包括完全自动化的证明系统和交互式证明助手，是形式化方法和人工智能的重要应用。
- English Definition: Automated theorem proving is the field of using computer programs to automatically generate or verify proofs of mathematical propositions, including fully automated proof systems and interactive proof assistants, an important application of formal methods and artificial intelligence.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ACM Computing Classification System: Theory of computation > Logic > Automated reasoning
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Automated Theorem Proving
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Automated theorem proving
  - Wikipedia: Automated theorem proving
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 自动定理证明虽然强大，但面临可扩展性和复杂性挑战
  - 形式化证明与人类直觉理解之间存在根本差异

### AI辅助证明 | AI-Assisted Proofs

- 中文定义：AI辅助证明是结合人工智能技术（如机器学习、神经网络）与传统形式化方法，辅助数学家发现证明思路、生成证明步骤或验证证明正确性的方法，代表了人机协作的数学推理新范式。
- English Definition: AI-assisted proofs are methods that combine artificial intelligence technologies (such as machine learning, neural networks) with traditional formal methods to assist mathematicians in discovering proof ideas, generating proof steps, or verifying proof correctness, representing a new paradigm of human-machine collaborative mathematical reasoning.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ACM Computing Classification System: Artificial intelligence > Knowledge representation and reasoning
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: AI-Assisted Mathematics
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Computer-assisted proofs
  - Wikipedia: Computer-assisted proof
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - AI辅助证明挑战了传统数学证明的本质和价值
  - 人机协作证明引发了关于数学理解与创造性的深刻讨论

### 神经符号推理 | Neuro-Symbolic Reasoning

- 中文定义：神经符号推理是结合神经网络的学习能力与符号逻辑的精确推理能力的人工智能方法，旨在克服纯神经网络和纯符号系统的局限性，为复杂推理任务提供更强大的解决方案。
- English Definition: Neuro-symbolic reasoning is an artificial intelligence method that combines the learning capabilities of neural networks with the precise reasoning capabilities of symbolic logic, aiming to overcome the limitations of pure neural networks and pure symbolic systems, providing more powerful solutions for complex reasoning tasks.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ACM Computing Classification System: Artificial intelligence > Neural networks
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Neural-Symbolic Computing
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Neural-symbolic integration
  - Wikipedia: Neural-symbolic computing
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 神经符号推理尝试弥合符号主义与连接主义的鸿沟
  - 知识表示与学习的统一是人工智能的核心哲学挑战之一

### 知识图谱 | Knowledge Graph

- 中文定义：知识图谱是一种结构化的知识表示方法，通过实体、关系和属性构建知识网络，支持复杂查询、推理和知识发现，为智能系统提供语义基础。
- English Definition: A knowledge graph is a structured knowledge representation method that builds knowledge networks through entities, relationships, and attributes, supporting complex queries, reasoning, and knowledge discovery, providing a semantic foundation for intelligent systems.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ACM Computing Classification System: Information systems > Information retrieval
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Knowledge Representation
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Knowledge representation
  - Wikipedia: Knowledge graph
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 知识图谱虽然提供了结构化表示，但面临知识获取和维护挑战
  - 知识表示的完备性与实用性之间存在根本张力

### 形式化证明 | Formal Proof

- 中文定义：形式化证明是在严格定义的形式系统中，按照明确的推理规则从公理出发逐步推导得出的完整证明，可以被计算机程序验证其正确性，是自动定理证明的基础。
- English Definition: A formal proof is a complete proof derived step by step from axioms according to well-defined inference rules in a strictly defined formal system, whose correctness can be verified by computer programs, forming the foundation of automated theorem proving.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ACM Computing Classification System: Theory of computation > Logic
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Formal Proof
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Formal proof
  - Wikipedia: Formal proof
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 形式化证明虽然严格，但可能缺乏直观理解和洞察
  - 形式与意义之间的关系是数学哲学的核心问题之一

### 大型语言模型 | Large Language Model

- 中文定义：大型语言模型是基于深度学习的大规模神经网络模型，通过预训练和微调可以理解、生成人类语言，在数学推理、证明生成和知识表示等任务中展现出强大能力。
- English Definition: Large language models are large-scale neural network models based on deep learning that can understand and generate human language through pre-training and fine-tuning, demonstrating powerful capabilities in mathematical reasoning, proof generation, and knowledge representation tasks.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ACM Computing Classification System: Computing methodologies > Machine learning
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Natural Language Processing
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Artificial intelligence in mathematics
  - Wikipedia: Large language model
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 大型语言模型在数学推理中的成功挑战了传统的数学能力观念
  - 模型的推理能力与真正理解之间的差异是认知科学的重要问题
