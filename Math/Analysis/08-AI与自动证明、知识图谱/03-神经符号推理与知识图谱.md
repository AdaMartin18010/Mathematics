## 概念定义 | Concept Definition

### 神经符号推理 | Neuro-symbolic Reasoning

- 中文定义：神经符号推理结合了神经网络的学习能力与符号推理的逻辑表达力，旨在实现可解释、可泛化的智能推理。知识图谱以图结构组织知识，支持符号推理与神经网络的融合应用，是智能系统的重要基础。
- English Definition: Neuro-symbolic reasoning combines the learning ability of neural networks with the logical expressiveness of symbolic reasoning, aiming for explainable and generalizable intelligent inference. Knowledge graphs organize knowledge in a graph structure, supporting the integration of symbolic reasoning and neural networks, and are a key foundation for intelligent systems.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Knowledge Graphs, Symbolic AI, Neural Networks
  - Wikipedia: Neuro-symbolic AI, Knowledge graph
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 神经符号推理试图弥合数据驱动与规则驱动AI的鸿沟，但融合机制、可解释性等仍是挑战
  - 知识图谱的动态演化、本体一致性与大规模推理能力是持续研究难题
  - 神经网络的"黑箱"特性与符号系统的透明性形成对比，推动了可解释AI的发展

### 神经网络 | Neural Networks

- 中文定义：神经网络是受生物神经系统启发的计算模型，通过多层神经元连接进行信息处理和模式识别。
- English Definition: Neural networks are computational models inspired by biological nervous systems, processing information and recognizing patterns through multi-layer neuron connections.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Neural Networks
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Neural Networks
  - Wikipedia: Artificial neural network
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 神经网络虽然具有强大的表示能力，但缺乏可解释性
  - 深度学习与符号推理之间存在张力

### 符号推理 | Symbolic Reasoning

- 中文定义：符号推理是基于逻辑规则和符号操作的推理方法，具有明确的语义和可解释性。
- English Definition: Symbolic reasoning is a reasoning method based on logical rules and symbolic operations, with clear semantics and interpretability.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Symbolic Reasoning
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Symbolic Reasoning
  - Wikipedia: Symbolic reasoning
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 符号推理虽然可解释，但可能缺乏灵活性
  - 符号系统与复杂现实之间存在张力

### 可微分推理 | Differentiable Reasoning

- 中文定义：可微分推理是将符号推理过程转化为可微分函数的方法，使神经网络能够学习推理规则。
- English Definition: Differentiable reasoning is a method of transforming symbolic reasoning processes into differentiable functions, enabling neural networks to learn reasoning rules.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Differentiable Reasoning
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Differentiable Reasoning
  - Wikipedia: Differentiable programming
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 可微分推理虽然结合了两种方法的优势，但可能增加系统复杂性
  - 可微分性与逻辑严谨性之间存在张力

### 知识图谱 | Knowledge Graph

- 中文定义：知识图谱是一种用于组织和表示知识的图结构技术，支持知识推理、问答和发现。
- English Definition: A knowledge graph is a graph-structured technology for organizing and representing knowledge, supporting knowledge reasoning, question answering, and discovery.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Knowledge Graph
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Knowledge Graph
  - Wikipedia: Knowledge graph
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 知识图谱虽然提供了结构化的知识表示，但可能简化了知识的复杂性
  - 图结构表示与知识的多维性之间存在张力

### 图神经网络 | Graph Neural Networks

- 中文定义：图神经网络是专门处理图结构数据的神经网络模型，能够学习节点和边的表示。
- English Definition: Graph neural networks are neural network models specifically designed to process graph-structured data, capable of learning representations of nodes and edges.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Graph Neural Networks
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Graph Neural Networks
  - Wikipedia: Graph neural network
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 图神经网络虽然适合图数据，但可能面临计算复杂性挑战
  - 图结构学习与可解释性之间存在张力
