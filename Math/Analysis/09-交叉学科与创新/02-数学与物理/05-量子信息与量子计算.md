## 概念定义 | Concept Definition

### 量子信息与量子计算 | Quantum Information & Quantum Computing

- 中文定义：量子信息是利用量子力学原理处理、传输和存储信息的科学。量子计算是利用量子力学现象（如叠加和纠缠）进行信息处理的计算范式。
- English Definition: Quantum information is the science of processing, transmitting, and storing information using quantum mechanical principles. Quantum computing is a computational paradigm that uses quantum mechanical phenomena such as superposition and entanglement for information processing.
- 国际标准/权威来源：

- 相关批判与哲学反思：

  - 量子与经典信息之间存在根本性差异
