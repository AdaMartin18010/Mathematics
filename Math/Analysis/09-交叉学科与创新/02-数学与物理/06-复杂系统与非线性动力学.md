## 概念定义 | Concept Definition

### 复杂系统与非线性动力学 | Complex Systems & Nonlinear Dynamics

- 中文定义：复杂系统是由大量相互作用的组件组成的系统，其整体行为不能从其组成部分的行为简单推导。非线性动力学研究系统状态随时间的演化，其中输出与输入不成正比关系。
- English Definition: Complex systems are systems composed of many interacting components whose overall behavior cannot be simply derived from the behavior of their constituent parts. Nonlinear dynamics studies the evolution of system states over time, where the output is not proportional to the input.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
