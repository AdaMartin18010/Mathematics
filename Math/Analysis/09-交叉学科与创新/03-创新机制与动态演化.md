## 概念定义 | Concept Definition

### 创新机制与动态演化 | Innovation Mechanisms & Dynamic Evolution

- 中文定义：创新机制是指推动新思想、新方法、新技术产生与扩散的系统过程。动态演化强调创新在时间和环境变化中的持续发展与适应。两者共同揭示了科学、技术、社会等领域创新的本质与规律。
- English Definition: Innovation mechanisms refer to the systematic processes that drive the generation and diffusion of new ideas, methods, and technologies. Dynamic evolution emphasizes the continuous development and adaptation of innovation over time and changing environments. Together, they reveal the essence and laws of innovation in science, technology, society, and beyond.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO 56000:2020 (Innovation management)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Innovation, Evolutionary Theory
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Innovation, Evolution
  - Wikipedia: Innovation, Evolutionary dynamics
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 创新机制需平衡理论深度与实际应用，防止"伪创新"与"碎片化"
  - 动态演化模型揭示了创新的非线性、复杂性与适应性
  - 创新与演化的伦理、规范与可持续性是未来研究的重要方向

### 系统论 | System Theory

- 中文定义：系统论是研究复杂系统结构、功能、演化和相互作用的跨学科理论，为理解创新机制提供了整体性视角。
- English Definition: System theory is an interdisciplinary theory that studies the structure, function, evolution, and interactions of complex systems, providing a holistic perspective for understanding innovation mechanisms.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO 56000:2020 (Innovation management)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: System Theory
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: System Theory
  - Wikipedia: Systems theory
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 系统论虽然提供了整体视角，但可能简化了系统复杂性
  - 系统思维与还原论之间存在张力

### 知识融合 | Knowledge Integration

- 中文定义：知识融合是将不同来源、不同形式的知识进行整合和创新的过程，旨在产生新的理解和洞察。
- English Definition: Knowledge integration is the process of integrating and innovating knowledge from different sources and forms, aiming to generate new understanding and insights.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO 56000:2020 (Innovation management)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Knowledge Integration
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Knowledge Integration
  - Wikipedia: Knowledge integration
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 知识融合虽然产生新见解，但可能面临一致性的挑战
  - 融合与分化之间存在张力

### 动态演化 | Dynamic Evolution

- 中文定义：动态演化是指系统随时间推移而发生的适应性变化和复杂性增长，包括结构演化、功能演化、关系演化等。
- English Definition: Dynamic evolution refers to adaptive changes and complexity growth of systems over time, including structural evolution, functional evolution, and relational evolution.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO 56000:2020 (Innovation management)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Dynamic Evolution
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Dynamic evolution
  - Wikipedia: Dynamic evolution
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 动态演化虽然揭示了变化规律，但可能面临预测困难
  - 演化与稳定性之间存在张力

### 协作机制 | Collaboration Mechanisms

- 中文定义：协作机制是指促进不同主体间有效合作与知识共享的制度安排和过程设计。
- English Definition: Collaboration mechanisms are institutional arrangements and process designs that promote effective cooperation and knowledge sharing among different entities.

### 知识图谱 | Knowledge Graph
