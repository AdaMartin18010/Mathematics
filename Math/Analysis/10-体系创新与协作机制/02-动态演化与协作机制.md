## 概念定义 | Concept Definition

### 动态演化与协作机制 | Dynamic Evolution & Collaboration Mechanisms

- 中文定义：动态演化机制描述系统在时间和环境变化下的持续发展与适应过程。协作机制是多主体通过组织、沟通与协同实现共同目标的系统方法。两者结合推动了复杂系统、科学研究、工程项目等领域的创新与进步。
- English Definition: Dynamic evolution mechanisms describe the continuous development and adaptation of systems under changing time and environments. Collaboration mechanisms are systematic approaches by which multiple agents achieve common goals through organization, communication, and coordination. Their combination drives innovation and progress in complex systems, scientific research, engineering projects, and more.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO 56000:2020 (Innovation management)
  - ISO 44001:2017 (Collaborative business relationship management)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Systems Theory, Collaboration, Evolutionary Theory
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Dynamic evolution, Collaboration
  - Wikipedia: Systems theory, Collaboration, Evolutionary dynamics
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 动态演化模型揭示了创新与协作的非线性、复杂性与适应性
  - 协作机制的有效性受限于组织结构、激励机制与沟通效率
  - 开放协作、跨界整合与动态演化是未来创新的重要趋势

### 系统动力学 | System Dynamics

- 中文定义：系统动力学是研究复杂系统随时间变化的动态行为的理论和方法，通过反馈循环和时变关系建模系统行为。
- English Definition: System dynamics is a theory and method for studying the dynamic behavior of complex systems over time, modeling system behavior through feedback loops and time-varying relationships.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO 56000:2020 (Innovation management)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: System Dynamics
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: System Dynamics
  - Wikipedia: System dynamics
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 系统动力学虽然提供了建模工具，但可能简化了系统复杂性
  - 模型与现实之间存在张力

### 自组织临界性 | Self-organized Criticality

- 中文定义：自组织临界性是指复杂系统在没有外部控制的情况下自发达到临界状态的现象，表现为幂律分布和分形结构。
- English Definition: Self-organized criticality is the phenomenon where complex systems spontaneously reach a critical state without external control, manifested as power-law distributions and fractal structures.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Self-organized Criticality
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Self-organized Criticality
  - Wikipedia: Self-organized criticality
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 自组织临界性虽然解释了复杂现象，但预测性有限
  - 临界性与稳定性之间存在张力

### 演化博弈论 | Evolutionary Game Theory

- 中文定义：演化博弈论是研究策略在群体中演化的理论，结合了博弈论和进化生物学的方法。
- English Definition: Evolutionary game theory is a theory that studies the evolution of strategies in populations, combining methods from game theory and evolutionary biology.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Evolutionary Game Theory
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Evolutionary Game Theory
  - Wikipedia: Evolutionary game theory
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 演化博弈论虽然提供了分析框架，但可能简化了人类行为
  - 理性假设与实际行为之间存在张力

### 多主体协作 | Multi-agent Collaboration

- 中文定义：多主体协作是指多个智能体通过协调和合作实现共同目标的过程，是分布式人工智能的重要研究领域。
- English Definition: Multi-agent collaboration is the process by which multiple intelligent agents achieve common goals through coordination and cooperation, an important research area in distributed artificial intelligence.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Multi-agent Systems
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Multi-agent Systems
  - Wikipedia: Multi-agent system
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 多主体协作虽然提高了效率，但可能面临协调复杂性
  - 个体理性与集体理性之间存在张力

### 复杂适应系统 | Complex Adaptive Systems

- 中文定义：复杂适应系统是指由多个相互作用的智能体组成的系统，能够通过学习和适应改变自身行为。
- English Definition: Complex adaptive systems are systems composed of multiple interacting intelligent agents that can change their behavior through learning and adaptation.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Complex Adaptive Systems
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Complex Adaptive Systems
  - Wikipedia: Complex adaptive system
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 复杂适应系统虽然解释了适应性，但可能面临预测困难
  - 适应性与稳定性之间存在张力

### 网络效应 | Network Effects

- 中文定义：网络效应是指产品或服务的价值随着用户数量增加而增加的现象。
- English Definition: Network effects are the phenomenon where the value of a product or service increases as the number of users increases.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Network Effects
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Network Effects
  - Wikipedia: Network effect
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 网络效应虽然解释了价值增长，但可能面临垄断问题
  - 规模效应与多样性之间存在张力
