## 概念定义 | Concept Definition

### 中断与持续执行计划 | Interruption & Continuous Execution Plan

- 中文定义：中断与持续执行计划是指在复杂任务或系统运行过程中，能够有效管理中断、记录进度、实现恢复与持续推进的机制。该机制广泛应用于项目管理、计算机系统、科学实验等领域，保障任务的可靠性与连续性。
- English Definition: Interruption and continuous execution planning refers to mechanisms that effectively manage interruptions, log progress, and enable recovery and continuous advancement during complex tasks or system operations. This mechanism is widely used in project management, computer systems, scientific experiments, etc., to ensure reliability and continuity of tasks.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO 56002:2019 (Innovation management — Innovation management system)
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Process Philosophy, Systems Theory
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Interruption, Continuous execution
  - Wikipedia: Fault tolerance, Checkpointing, Project management
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 中断管理与持续执行机制提升了系统的鲁棒性，但也增加了复杂性与管理成本
  - 断点恢复、进度日志等机制需兼顾效率、可追溯性与安全性
  - 持续推进与灵活应对变化之间的平衡是系统设计的重要课题

### 断点续传 | Checkpointing

- 中文定义：断点续传是指在系统运行过程中定期保存状态，以便在中断后能够从保存点恢复执行的技术。
- English Definition: Checkpointing is a technique for periodically saving system state during operation so that execution can resume from the saved point after interruption.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Checkpointing
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Checkpointing
  - Wikipedia: Checkpointing
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 断点续传虽然提高了可靠性，但可能影响性能
  - 状态保存与执行效率之间存在张力

### 进度日志 | Progress Logging

- 中文定义：进度日志是指记录任务执行过程和关键节点的系统化方法，用于追踪进度、分析问题和优化流程。
- English Definition: Progress logging is a systematic method for recording task execution processes and key milestones, used for tracking progress, analyzing problems, and optimizing processes.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO 56002:2019 (Innovation management — Innovation management system)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Progress Logging
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Progress Logging
  - Wikipedia: Progress logging
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 进度日志虽然提供了可追溯性，但可能增加管理负担
  - 详细记录与简洁性之间存在张力

### 容错机制 | Fault Tolerance

- 中文定义：容错机制是指系统在部分组件失效时仍能继续正常运行的能力，通过冗余、检测和恢复等技术实现。
- English Definition: Fault tolerance is the ability of a system to continue normal operation when some components fail, achieved through redundancy, detection, and recovery techniques.
- 国际标准/权威来源：
  - ISO/IEC 2382:2015 (Information technology — Vocabulary)
  - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Fault Tolerance
  - Encyclopedia of Mathematics: Fault Tolerance
  - Wikipedia: Fault tolerance
- 相关批判与哲学反思：
  - 容错机制虽然提高了可靠性，但可能增加系统复杂性
  - 冗余与效率之间存在张力

### 持续集成 | Continuous Integration

- 中文定义：持续集成是指频繁地将代码集成到共享仓库中，通过自动化测试和构建确保代码质量的方法。
- English Definition: Continuous integration is a method of frequently integrating code into a shared repository, ensuring code quality through automated testing and building.
- 国际标准/权威来源：

### 动态调度 | Dynamic Scheduling

- 中文定义：动态调度是指根据系统状态和资源情况实时调整任务执行顺序和分配的技术。
- English Definition: Dynamic scheduling is a technique for real-time adjustment of task execution order and allocation based on system state and resource conditions.

  - 灵活性与稳定性之间存在张力

### 版本控制 | Version Control

- 中文定义：版本控制是指管理和跟踪文件或代码变更历史的系统，支持协作开发和回滚操作。
